With all the buzz around agents, LLMs, and the tools they power, it’s sometimes easy (or at least tempting) to think that fundamental machine learning workflows—feature selection, model monitoring, and the like—will soon become obsolete.

The articles we selected for you this week paint a different and more nuanced picture. Sure, practitioners have powerful new toys to play with, but the inherent value in understanding how models work (and break) isn’t going anywhere. Read on to learn how you can continue to develop as a data or ML professional in the “AI is everywhere!” age.
