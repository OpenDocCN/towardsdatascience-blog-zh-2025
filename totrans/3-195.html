Other Recommended Reads

From the social impact of generative AI to cutting-edge techniques for synthetic-data creation, these standout articles cover a wide range of timely topics with depth and nuance.

  • Learn about the most powerful ways to customize LLMs for enhanced performance — Destin Gong walks us through no fewer than six.
  • What happens if we “pivot too hard in the direction of efficiency and lose sight of the end goal of effectiveness”? Stephanie Kirmer takes an incisive look at the potential havoc generative-AI tools might wreak on civic institutions. 
  • If you’d like to expand your fundamental understanding of large language models, the latest installment in Clara Chong‘s series covers reinforcement learning, DeepSeek, and more.
  • Given the major progress we see in agentic AI tools, Gadi Singer argues that it’s more important than ever to integrate alignment technologies into their development.
  • Take synthetic-data generation to the next level by following along Torty Sivill‘s detailed guide, which relies on the power of variational autoencoders.
