Summer slowdown? Not at TDS. As new and exciting developments continued to unfold in the world of large language models and AI agents, our authors shared smart and actionable insights on cutting-edge tools and approaches, and published some of our most widely circulating articles of the year.

Read on to discover — or revisit — July’s most popular stories. They cover the inner workings of agentic-AI workflows and Python optimization (among many other topics), and also include a cluster of top-notch reflections on data and ML careers in a rapidly changing job landscape.

A Developer’s Guide to Building Scalable AI: Workflows vs Agents

As Haily Quach explains in our most-read article of the past month, it’s crucial to understand the architectural trade-offs between autonomous agents and orchestrated workflows — and practitioners on the ground (like you!) stand to gain a lot by making informed product decisions.

How to Fine-Tune Small Language Models to Think with Reinforcement Learning

In the mood for a technical deep dive? Avishek Biswas delivers an excellent guide to SLM fine-tuning with PyTorch.
