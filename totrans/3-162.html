Should Data Scientists Care About Quantum Computing?

As Sara Metwalli reminds us, 2025 was declared the year of quantum information science—so what better opportunity for data practitioners to learn about this increasingly buzz-generating field? Read her accessible introduction to learn how it might impact the future of data-focused workflows.

Essential Review Papers on Physics-Informed Neural Networks: A Curated Guide for Practitioners

Shuai Guo presents a comprehensive resource to help you catch up with the latest research in a cutting-edge machine learning subfield.

Unraveling Spatially Variable Genes: A Statistical Perspective on Spatial Transcriptomics

Explore the innovative use of data and statistics in genomics: Guanao Yan and Jingyi Jessica Li share their latest findings.

Other Recommended Reads

  • In a focused and practical guide, Claudia Ng outlines three essential methods to track model drift.
  • What’s behind the recent trend of companies moving back to on-premise servers and opting out of cloud services? Ari Joury unpacks some of the key drivers.
